Course Introduction

Course Title: Mastering Productivity

This course is designed to help you build consistent productivity habits through a guided 3-day challenge. Each level focuses on a core area of improvement and unlocks after 24 hours.

Level 1: Focus and Clarity

Learn techniques to eliminate distractions and stay mentally clear.

Level 2: Time Management

Understand how to organize your time and set realistic goals.

Level 3: Discipline and Consistency

Build routines and systems to stay productive long-term.
